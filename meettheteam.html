Meet The Team

The Lux Fitness Crew

Kareem Hallak

Hi, I'm Kareem... With over five years of dedicated experience as a personal trainer, I, Kareem, have devoted my life to changing others' lives through fitness and nutrition. My journey in the fitness world is not just a career; it's a deep-seated passion for inspiring individuals to surpass their perceived limitations and to become the best versions of themselves. My philosophy revolves around the belief that everyone has an untapped reservoir of potential. By guiding my clients through tailored fitness and nutrition plans, I strive to unlock this potential. My experience in competing in both classic physique and men's physique competitions has not only honed my skills but also imbued me with an in-depth understanding of the demands of intense training and dieting. This journey has taught me the importance of balance and sustainability in fitness routines. I understand the hardships that come with strict diets and rigorous training schedules. Leveraging this knowledge, I have developed an approach to simplify and effectively integrate nutrition and workout plans into diverse lifestyles, ensuring they are not just effective but also enjoyable and manageable. Your fitness journey is deeply personal, and as your coach, I am here to navigate it with you. Together, we will break barriers, set new benchmarks, and celebrate every victory on your path to becoming the best version of yourself.

Rita Marie Bastone

Hi ! I am a dedicated practitioner with a dual expertise as a massage therapist and an osteopath, complemented by my passion for fitness and strength training. My journey into the world of health and wellness began with my own transformation in the gym. The discipline and resilience I cultivated there led me to explore the profound impact of therapeutic touch and structural alignment on overall well-being. As a massage therapist, I harness the power of touch to release tension and promote relaxation, aiding my clients in their pursuit of a pain-free life. My approach is a blend of precision and intuition, ensuring a personalized experience that addresses the unique needs of each individual. Expanding my horizons, I delved into osteopathy, where I apply a comprehensive understanding of the body’s interconnected systems to facilitate healing. My practice is grounded in the philosophy that the body has an innate ability to heal itself, and my role is to support and enhance this natural process. Through my work, I strive to be more than just a practitioner; I aim to be a guide, an educator, and a partner in my clients’ journeys toward optimal health.
